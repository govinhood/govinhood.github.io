Hello World !!

My Name is Govinda Sharma.I'm a professional photographer- aerial photography, night photography, Astro photography, portrait photography, wildlife photography.
Photography is my muse, my true passion, and the thing that will undoubtedly drive me insane someday.
I dont photograph things. I photograph the way they make me feel. Amittedely, it's a bit of strange concept.
But it's honest and it's the best way to describe my approach to the craft. I wrestle with every image I shoot. I assume perfection is possible.
I have worked for some insanely powerful and boring companies. I have taught over 200 people in photography & digital design.
I have worked in all extremes, from photographing diamonds deep under in an freezing temperature, to chasing ancient shadows in the palaces and Valley of Kathmandu.
I haven't won awards but that isn't what matters. What matters is chasing the light and sharing it. :)

Education

I enjoyed my childhood in India and later I got chance to live and study in a beautiful country - Nepal.
I graduated High School from Nepalgunj, the most famous Western City of Nepal. Most of the time I explore new places and made a plan to explore the Capital city, the city of temples, Kathmandu.

I joined University as well as started working as a professional photographer since 2014.
I am graduated in Masters in Photography from St. Xavier's College in 2016.

Social Networks

The web has been a popular medium for storing and sharing photos ever since the first photograph was published. Today photo sharing sites such as Flickr, Picasa, and PhotoBucket, as well as social Web sites, are used by millions of people to share their pictures.

In today's world digital photography and social media websites allow organizations and corporations to make photographs more accessible to a greater and more diverse population. For example, National Geographic Magazine has a Twitter, Snapchat, Facebook, and Instagram accounts and each one includes content aimed for the type of audience that are part of each social media community.
